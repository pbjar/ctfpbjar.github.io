Some info about CTFs...

  • What is a CTF?

    Well, a CTF (Capture the Flag) is a cybersecurity competition that focuses on a variety of topics. This includes Web Exploitation, Binary Exploitation, Forensics, Cryptography, Reversing and so much more. CTFs test you on these skills by providing you with fun and interactive challenges, where the goal is to capture a flag. A flag looks very similar to flag{hi_i_am_a_flag}.   • HALPPPPPP I've never done a CTF before?!?!?!?!?!

    Woah...Woah...Woah...don't worry. This CTF will test people from all difficulty ranges. We want to be as inclusive as possible with how hard our challenges are. This means there will be a plethora of problems that a geared to those who start CTFs for the first time, and there will be just as many for those who are regulars. We want our CTF to boost CTF morale in general. This means those who are long-time competitors and those who have made this CTF their first CTF, really enjoy the competition. This does not mean...all problems will be easy :p
